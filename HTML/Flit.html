Pioneering Mosquito Protection from Indoors to Outdoors

Advanced mosquito protection for every space—indoors and outdoors. With long-lasting defense that goes beyond repellence, we keep your family safe, healthy, and worry-free.

FLIT keeps your home and outdoor spaces mosquito-free, providing reliable protection so your family can enjoy comfort, safety, and peace of mind every day.

We believe a safe home begins with effective protection. A mosquito-free environment nurtures comfort and peace of mind, and this balance is best achieved through solutions that are both powerful and reliable. Each FLIT product embodies this philosophy, delivering an impeccable blend of effectiveness, safety, and ease of use.

At FLIT, we uphold the principle that protection and care go hand in hand. When mosquito control is strong yet safe, your home and loved ones remain worry-free. This is the experience our customers value and the reason they trust FLIT, day after day.

From formulation to every spray, we pioneer next-generation mosquito management solutions that combine innovation, reliability, and care to keep your family safe, comfortable, and happy.
