Pioneering Nutrition Solutions from Field to Formulation

Moi Forest brings you the purity of nature in every drop. Crafted with care, our personal care products nurture your skin and wellbeing, naturally.

Moi Forest is inspired by the wisdom of ancient forests — guardians of biodiversity and the very foundation of our planet’s habitability. Within these forests lie shelter, nourishment, and balance: trees, berries, mushrooms, and above all, a living ecosystem that breathes life into everything around it.

From this inspiration, a group of Finnish researchers, together with women passionate about natural cosmetics and wellbeing, asked a simple but powerful question: Could the essence of the forest be harnessed to create cosmetics that truly benefit the whole body?

The answer became Moi Forest. By combining organic, nutrient-rich plant oils with our unique magical Forest Dust®, we craft products that deliver superior care — especially for sensitive skin. Each formula is designed as a synergistic whole, working in harmony with the skin while ensuring minimal impact on nature.

At Moi Forest, we bring the vitality of the forest into your daily rituals, so you can nurture your skin and honour the planet at the same time.
