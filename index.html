Our Core Categories

Food

Only a healthy body can house a healthy mind—and the journey begins with food that is nourishing, natural, and wholesome. Ayush Vedica offers a power-packed combination of flavour, nutrition, and everyday convenience through its carefully curated range of natural foods.

Oils

Discover the purity and flavour of Ayush Vedica’s mustard oil range—Kachi Ghani, First Pressed (Pheli Dhaar), and Yellow Mustard (Peeli Sarso)—crafted using traditional cold-press methods to retain natural pungency, nutrients, and aroma. While Kachi Ghani offers a bold, robust flavour ideal for pickles and curries, First Wave delivers the purest first-press extract, and Yellow Mustard provides a lighter taste for everyday cooking. All variants offer high oxidative stability, ensuring long-lasting freshness and quality.

Mosquito & fly killer

At FLIT, we believe a healthy life begins with a safe and protected home. Our mosquito management solutions are thoughtfully formulated to provide powerful protection while staying true to nature. Using plant-based ingredients and proven methods, FLIT products effectively repel mosquitoes without harsh chemicals.

Laundry & home care

Our brand FEWA is an integral to the daily lives of our consumers. We are committed to driving the next generation of sustainable product solutions, aligned with evolving consumer behaviors and expectations. Through close collaboration with consumers and retail partners, we continuously develop market-leading innovations across key categories, including laundry care, dishwashing, surface cleaning, and toilet care.

Personal Care

At Moi Forest, we believe beauty is all about the details that inspire confidence and self-expression. Our products are crafted to blend quality, care, and style—designed to make everyday routines effortless while adding a touch of creativity and elegance.
